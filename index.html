Recreational Dive Limits

  • Maximum Depth: 130 feet (40 meters) for recreational diving
  • Recommended Beginner Depth: 60 feet (18 meters)
  • Ascent Rate: No faster than 30 feet/minute (9 meters/minute)
  • Safety Stop: 3-5 minutes at 15 feet (5 meters)
  • Minimum Surface Interval: Plan appropriate surface intervals between dives

GUE Technical Diving Standards

  • Standard Gases: GUE uses standardized gas mixes for specific depth ranges
  • Gradient Factors: Conservative 30/70 or 30/85 depending on conditions
  • Ascent Rate: 30 feet/minute (10 meters/minute)
  • Deep Stops: Integrated through gradient factor methodology
  • Min Deco: Minimum decompression time approach
  • Team Diving: All technical dives require proper team configuration
  • Gas Planning: Rule of thirds for backmount, rule of sixths for sidemount

Always dive with a buddy and within your certification limits.
