Important Safety Information

  • Maximum Depth: Recreational diving limit is 40m (130ft)
  • Safety Stop: Always perform a 3-5 minute stop at 5m (15ft)
  • Ascent Rate: Never exceed 9m/min (30ft/min)
  • Reserve Air: End dive with at least 50 bar (700 psi)
  • Buddy System: Never dive alone
  • Dive Tables: This calculator uses simplified PADI recreational dive tables
