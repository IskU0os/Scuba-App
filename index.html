Recreational Dive Limits

  • Maximum Depth: 130 feet (40 meters) for recreational diving
  • Recommended Beginner Depth: 60 feet (18 meters)
  • Ascent Rate: No faster than 30 feet/minute (9 meters/minute)
  • Safety Stop: 3-5 minutes at 15 feet (5 meters)
  • Minimum Surface Interval: Plan appropriate surface intervals between dives

Always dive with a buddy and within your certification limits.
